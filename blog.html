> Today I learned
29 Mar 2025
The Swiss company known as "Proton" which many users flock too for its privacy, is a subsediary of its own non-profit Proton Foundation. The reason for this majority share owner being a non-profit is that Proton cannot be bought out by any other company, so the company can continue to focus on its "privacy first" mission.
