"The buzzer" is a radio station in Russia, it is a shortwave station that broadcasts a monotonous buzzing tone. 24 hours a day this station is on, however sometimes this is interrupted by transmissions in Russian. It has been speculated to be part of the "dead hand" system, however this has been proven false by the fact it has been interrupted many times.
